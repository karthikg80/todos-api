Get tasks out of your head, fast

Type naturally — "Call dentist tomorrow 2pm" — and the app figures out the date. Organize into projects, set priorities, and reorder with drag-and-drop.

AI that helps you plan, not just list

Get instant feedback on vague tasks, turn goals into step-by-step plans, and break big projects into actionable pieces — all powered by AI.

Chat with AI to manage your tasks

Connect Claude or ChatGPT and manage your tasks through conversation. "What's due this week?" or "Add a task to buy groceries" — just ask.

Daily planning, weekly reviews, and stale task cleanup run automatically in the background. Wake up to a prioritized day, every day.
